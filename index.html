About Me

Hello! Thank you so much for viewing my website. Let me tell you a little bit about myself. My name is Jeff Taylor and I live in Raleigh, NC. I have most recently been a student in the Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, an intensive 24-week long program dedicated to designing and building web applications.

Prior to that, I held Senior Applications Engineering positions at both Lenovo and IBM where I developed and mastered a deep understanding of printed circuit boards culminating in my position as the lead subject matter expert.

Throughout my career I have always found that my greatest love and highest motivation has been in the solving of complex problems as well as in the opportunity to learn new skills and become proficient in their application. So it is a natural progression for me to pursue an interest in software development that I have had since college. I am excited about the opportunity to further develop my coding skills and to add new skills to my arsenal while helping to solve problems.
